First Time?

What to Expect

We want your first visit to feel easy and comfortable. Here’s a quick look at what a Sunday morning looks like at True Light.

🎵

Worship & Music

Our services open with 20–25 minutes of contemporary worship music. Expect uplifting songs, live instrumentation, and a warm, Spirit-filled atmosphere. Sing along, raise your hands, or simply take it in — there’s no wrong way to worship.

📖

The Message

After worship, our pastor delivers a 35–40 minute Bible-based message. Teaching is practical, grounded in Scripture, and designed to meet you where you are. We encourage you to bring a Bible or follow along on your phone.

👕

What to Wear

Come casual. Seriously — jeans, sneakers, whatever you’re comfortable in. We care far more about you being present than what you’re wearing. You’ll see everything from Sunday best to athleisure in our congregation.

🅿️

Parking

Free parking is available in our main lot off Worship Lane. Look for our parking team in bright yellow vests — they’ll guide you right in. Overflow parking is available in the adjacent lot on Sunday mornings. Accessible spaces are located near the main entrance.

Families Welcome

Kids & Youth

We believe every generation deserves a place to encounter God. Your kids are not just welcome — they’re expected.

🧒

True Light Kids (Ages 0–12)

From our nursery for infants to our elementary-age classrooms, True Light Kids offers age-appropriate Bible lessons, worship, games, and community. Every volunteer is background-checked and trained to create a safe, nurturing environment.

  • Nursery: Ages 0–2
  • Preschool: Ages 3–5
  • Elementary: Grades K–5
🙌

True Light Youth (Ages 13–18)

Our youth ministry is a place where teenagers can be real, ask hard questions, build genuine friendships, and grow in their faith. We meet during Sunday services and host regular events, retreats, and outreach opportunities throughout the year.

  • Middle School: Grades 6–8
  • High School: Grades 9–12
  • Weekly hangouts & monthly events
🔒

Safe & Secure Check-In

We use a secure check-in system for all children. Parents receive a matching tag at drop-off and must present it at pick-up. Your child’s safety is our top priority.

Common Questions

FAQ

A few things people often wonder before their first visit.

❓ Do I need to be a Christian to attend?

Not at all. Whether you’re exploring faith for the first time, returning after a long break, or simply curious — you are welcome here. We’re a community of people at all different stages of their spiritual journey.

❓ Will I be put on the spot or asked to stand up?

Never. We know visiting a new church can feel vulnerable. We won’t ask first-time guests to raise their hand, stand up, or introduce themselves publicly. Just come, sit back, and experience the service at your own pace.

❓ How long is the service?

Our Sunday services run approximately 75–90 minutes. Wednesday night Bible study is typically 60–75 minutes. We start and end on time so you can plan accordingly.

❓ Is there a place to get coffee before service?

Yes! Our Welcome Café opens 30 minutes before each Sunday service. Grab a coffee, meet some people, and get settled before things kick off. It’s one of our favorite parts of Sunday morning.

❓ What if I have more questions before I visit?

We’d love to hear from you. Reach out through our contact form or stop by the Welcome Desk when you arrive — our team is there specifically to help first-time guests feel at home.
