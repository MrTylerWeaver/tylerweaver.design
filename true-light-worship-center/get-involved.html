Baptism & Membership

💧

Baptism

Baptism is a public declaration of your faith in Jesus Christ — an outward expression of an inward transformation. If you’ve placed your trust in Jesus and haven’t been baptized, we’d love to celebrate that step with you.

We offer baptism services regularly throughout the year. Talk to a pastor or fill out the form below to get started.

Request Baptism
🏠

Church Membership

Membership at True Light is a covenant commitment — to this community, to its mission, and to one another. Members attend our Next Steps class, affirm our statement of faith, and commit to giving, serving, and growing together.

Our Next Steps class is offered monthly and is the gateway to membership. We’d love to have you join the family.
